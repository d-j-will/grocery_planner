6 · One degradation policy for the AI seam

Worth exploring ports & adapters

process_receipt.ex:71 · categorizer.ex:72,128 · receipt.ex:95 · application.ex:58 · receipt_live.ex:547,937,1054

Before · two contradictory definitions of "down"

After · one policy owns "unavailable"

Problem
The optional-sidecar seam has no single owner of "unavailable": categorization degrades cleanly, extraction hard-fails and burns retries, and each caller invents its own message.

Solution
One available? policy so extraction short-circuits to :awaiting_ai and views share one message.

  • locality: one down-policy
  • stops burning Oban attempts
  • receipts recoverable, not failed
  • one degraded message, not three
Finding is a missing seam, not a shallow one — nothing to delete; the policy simply doesn't exist as a module yet.
